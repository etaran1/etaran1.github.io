Suspicious PowerShell Activity Investigation

Role Simulation: SOC Analyst I
SOC / Triage
Scenario

Endpoint telemetry triggered on unusual PowerShell behavior. Goal: validate if activity was benign admin behavior or malicious execution.

What I Did
  • Validated alert context (host, user, parent process, command line, and timing).
  • Checked for common abuse patterns (encoded commands, download cradles, LOLBins).
  • Correlated with adjacent signals (logon type, process tree, network connections).
  • Documented triage decision and next-step recommendations.
Outcome

Triaged and classified with a clear rationale and evidence references, plus escalation criteria if additional indicators appeared.

Brute-Force Login Attempt with Lateral Movement Risk

Role Simulation: SOC Analyst II
SOC / Escalation
Scenario

Multiple failed authentication attempts across accounts and hosts suggested credential guessing and potential movement between systems.

What I Did
  • Assessed severity using volume, spread, and success indicators.
  • Identified impacted identities, target systems, and attack pattern.
  • Recommended containment steps (account controls, IP blocks, segmentation checks).
  • Prepared escalation package with business impact + required actions.
Outcome

Escalated with a structured summary, clear risk narrative, and actionable containment recommendations aligned to SOC workflows.
