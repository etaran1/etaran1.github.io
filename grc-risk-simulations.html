Insecure Data Storage Risk Assessment

Role Simulation: GRC / Risk Analyst
GRC / Risk
Scenario

Sensitive operational data stored on shared network drives without encryption or regular access review.

Analysis & Decisions
  • Identified confidentiality and compliance exposure.
  • Assessed likelihood based on access scope and audit gaps.
  • Rated impact as high due to data sensitivity.
  • Selected mitigation over acceptance.
Risk decision: Not acceptable as-is due to broad access and high confidentiality impact.
Outcome
  • Risk logged and prioritized for remediation.
  • Encryption-at-rest and quarterly access reviews recommended.
Artifacts Produced
Risk Register Entry Likelihood × Impact Scoring Mitigation Plan
Reflection
  • Data classification simplifies scoring consistency.
  • Automated access reporting improves audit quality.

Third-Party Vendor Access Risk Evaluation

Role Simulation: GRC / Risk Analyst
GRC / Vendor
Scenario

A third-party vendor requests persistent access to internal systems for support operations.

Analysis & Decisions
  • Reviewed access scope, authentication strength, and monitoring.
  • Identified over-privilege and lack of time-bound controls.
  • Conditionally accepted risk with compensating controls.
  • Required time-bound access and review cadence.
Residual risk acceptable only with segmentation, monitoring, and periodic review.
Outcome
  • Risk accepted with documented conditions.
  • Vendor access redesigned to least privilege.
Artifacts Produced
Risk Acceptance Memo Control Justification Notes Residual Risk Assessment
Reflection
  • Segmentation reduces vendor blast radius.
  • Logging improves incident response readiness.
