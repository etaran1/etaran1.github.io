Insecure Data Storage Risk Assessment

Role Simulation: GRC / Risk Analyst
GRC / Risk
Scenario

Sensitive operational data stored on shared network drives without encryption or periodic access review.

Analysis & Decisions
  • Assessed confidentiality and compliance exposure based on data sensitivity.
  • Rated likelihood using access scope, role distribution, and audit gaps.
  • Prioritized mitigation over acceptance due to impact severity.
  • Proposed compensating controls: encryption + access review cadence.
Risk decision: Not acceptable as-is. Mitigation required due to broad access scope and high confidentiality impact.
Outcome
  • Risk documented, scored, and prioritized for remediation.
  • Recommended encryption-at-rest and quarterly access audits.
Artifacts Produced
Risk Register Entry Likelihood × Impact Scoring Mitigation Recommendation
Reflection
  • Formal data classification reduces scoring ambiguity.
  • Automated access reporting improves audit quality and cadence.

Third-Party Vendor Access Risk Evaluation

Role Simulation: GRC / Risk Analyst
GRC / Vendor
Scenario

Third-party vendor requests persistent network access for operational support.

Analysis & Decisions
  • Assessed vendor access scope, authentication strength, and monitoring gaps.
  • Identified over-privilege risk and weak time-bound controls.
  • Conditionally accepted risk with compensating controls and monitoring.
  • Recommended time-bound access, segmentation, and periodic review.
Residual risk approach: Acceptable only with compensating controls (time-bound access + monitoring + review cadence).
Outcome
  • Risk accepted with documented conditions and control requirements.
  • Proposed vendor access review cycle and least-privilege redesign.
Artifacts Produced
Risk Acceptance Memo Control Justification Notes Residual Risk Assessment
Reflection
  • Segmented vendor access reduces blast radius immediately.
  • Strong logging requirements improve incident response readiness.
